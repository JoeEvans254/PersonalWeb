My Childhood in Nairobi, Kamorock

I was born and raised in the vibrant city of Nairobi, specifically in the neighborhood of Kamorock. Some of my fondest childhood memories include:

  • Playing soccer with friends in the local field.
  • Exploring the nearby forests and pretending to be on adventures.
  • Attending community events and festivals celebrating our diverse culture.
  • Helping out at my family's small business in the local market.
